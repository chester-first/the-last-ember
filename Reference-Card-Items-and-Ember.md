## YOUR THEME'S TRACK — copy your five powers onto your card above

*(Find your item's theme; write its line into each milestone slot. "+X" = bonus to attack/save
DC if it's a weapon or focus.)*

### ⚔ PYRE-BLADE — a martial weapon
- **Kindled:** +1d4 fire on hits; sheds 10 ft of light you control.
- **Burning (+1):** 1/short rest, a hit *returns* a slain undead's soul and heals you 1d8.
- **Blazing (+2):** on a crit, +3d6 radiant and the target can't regain HP until your next turn.
- **Radiant (+3):** 1/day **Dawnstroke** — one attack does +8d6 radiant; undead under half HP
  must save (DC 18 Con) or be destroyed.
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, **catch a falling ally's soul** the instant they die — back at 1 HP.

### 🛡 WARDENS' AEGIS — a shield or armor
- **Kindled:** +1 AC vs. undead; advantage on saves vs. fear.
- **Burning (+1 shield):** reaction 1/short rest to halve necrotic damage to an ally within 5 ft.
- **Blazing (+2):** allies within 10 ft resist soul-draining "max-HP-loss" effects.
- **Radiant (+3):** 1/day raise a 15-ft **Ember Bulwark** wall (total cover, 1 min).
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, **cancel one creature's death** within 30 ft — no save.

### 🏮 SOUL-LANTERN — a holy symbol or focus
- **Kindled:** *light* at will; you always sense undead within 30 ft.
- **Burning (save DC +1):** 1/short rest, *return* a destroyed undead's soul + grant an ally
  temp HP = your level.
- **Blazing (+2):** your healing pushes back the Dimming in a region (ask your GM).
- **Radiant (+3):** 1/day a column of true dawn (*sunbeam*) that *returns* every soul it destroys.
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, **open a path to the Source** — the way home for the truly lost.

### 🎵 GRAVESONG — an instrument or a rogue's blade
- **Kindled:** speak with the recently dead, 1/day.
- **Burning (+1):** 1/short rest, *talk down* an undead or restless soul (Wis save) instead of
  fighting it.
- **Blazing (+2):** advantage with the dead and the fey; learn a secret from any soul you return.
- **Radiant (+3):** 1/day **Chorus of Release** — up to 6 souls within 30 ft save or peacefully
  return.
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, **turn one enemy lieutenant** back to your side for one battle.

### 🐉 WYRMSCALE TOKEN — a draconic relic (any caster)
- **Kindled:** resistance to one damage type (your "dragon" — you pick).
- **Burning (+1 spell atk):** 1/short rest add 1d8 of your type to a spell.
- **Blazing (+2):** 1/day breathe a 30-ft cone of your element (8d6).
- **Radiant (+3):** gain a flying speed; frightful presence vs. undead.
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, briefly **become a true gold dragon** — the hero's answer to the fall.

### 🪙 QUIET HEIRLOOM — for the PC who'd rather not fight
- **Kindled:** 1/day reroll a failed save (keep the new roll); it warms when a loved one's in danger.
- **Burning:** advantage on checks to sway people's loyalties and steady fraying bonds.
- **Blazing:** 1/day grant an ally inspiration *and* immunity to fear for a scene.
- **Radiant:** 1/day, when an ally would hit 0 HP, they drop to half instead.
- **True Ember:** 1/campaign, **guarantee one person you love a future** — whatever ending comes.
