# World of Darkness — Setting Comparison & Ratings

*All current lines included. Ranked by popularity + critical standing + thematic strength. **(oWoD)** = Classic/Old World of Darkness · **(CofD)** = Chronicles of Darkness.*

---

### ★★★★★ — 5/5

**Vampire: The Masquerade (oWoD)**
The flagship. A newly-dead predator clings to humanity while clan politics and the Beast inside try to take it. Most popular line in the franchise; current **5th Edition (V5)**.
*Themes: predator vs. person · addiction · power's cost · conspiracy · immortality as curse.*

---

### ★★★★½ — 4.5/5

**Werewolf: The Apocalypse (oWoD)**
A shapeshifting holy warrior in a doomed last stand against corruption (the Wyrm) and ecological collapse. Rage, tribe, righteous war; current **5th Edition (W5)**.
*Themes: eco-apocalypse · sacred duty · rage · spirit & flesh · inevitable loss.*

---

### ★★★★ — 4/5

**Mage: The Ascension (oWoD)**
Reality is a consensus you can break. Your enlightened belief reshapes the world — with Paradox backlash — while the Technocracy enforces "normal."
*Themes: belief vs. reality · free will vs. control · science vs. mysticism.*

**Hunter: The Reckoning (oWoD / current 5e)**
An ordinary mortal who's seen the monsters and fights back — outgunned and possibly losing their mind. The most accessible modern entry point.
*Themes: ordinary courage · paranoia · the cost of the crusade.*

---

### ★★★½ — 3.5/5

**Vampire: The Requiem (CofD)**
The reboot of Vampire — cleaner mechanics, a build-your-own-metaplot sandbox. Often called the mechanically superior vampire game.
*Themes: predation · conscience · politics · existential ambiguity.*

**Mage: The Awakening (CofD)**
Atlantean sorcerers drawing on a fallen supernal world. More systematized and consistent than Ascension.
*Themes: hubris · the seeker's curse · secrets & gnosis.*

**Wraith: The Oblivion (oWoD)**
A ghost in the Shadowlands bound by Passions and Fetters, fighting your own dark Shadow while Oblivion claws at everything. Famously bleak, emotionally brutal, cult-revered — and hard to run.
*Themes: grief · unfinished business · self-destruction · the war within.*

**Demon: The Descent (CofD)**
A rogue angel of the God-Machine who "fell" and now lives as a fugitive spy juggling cover identities and dwindling humanity.
*Themes: identity & deception · free will vs. the machine · the price of defiance.*

**Changeling: The Dreaming (oWoD)**
A faerie soul reborn in a mortal body, holding onto wonder (Glamour) against a gray world of disbelief (Banality). Whimsical, bittersweet, divisive.
*Themes: wonder vs. cynicism · the death of childhood · art & dream.*

---

### ★★★ — 3/5

**Changeling: The Lost (CofD)**
Abducted by inhuman Fae, you escaped after years of torment — and something wearing your face took your place. Dark, horror-forward.
*Themes: trauma & recovery · captivity · stolen identity · the bargain's price.*

**Werewolf: The Forsaken (CofD)**
Spirit-world police walking the border of flesh and spirit. Personal, local horror over doomed eco-war.
*Themes: duty & territory · the hunt · family & pack.*

**Hunter: The Vigil (CofD)**
Mortals facing the supernatural from neighborhood cells to global conspiracies. Flexible, tier-based.
*Themes: knowledge as burden · escalation · defender to zealot.*

**Promethean: The Created (CofD)**
An artificial human on a pilgrimage to earn a real soul — while your existence poisons the world around you. Tragic cult favorite.
*Themes: what makes us human · alienation · redemption through suffering.*

**Demon: The Fallen (oWoD)**
A fallen angel of Lucifer's rebellion returned to possess a human host, remembering Heaven and torn between torment and redemption.
*Themes: faith & damnation · memory of grace · ends vs. means.*

**Mummy: The Curse (CofD)**
One of the Arisen — a deathless servant of the Judges of Duat, awakening across millennia, driven by Sekhem and bound to relics and a half-forgotten purpose.
*Themes: eternal servitude · memory & decay · duty across ages.*

---

### ★★½ — 2.5/5

**Geist: The Sin-Eaters (CofD)**
You died, bargained with a death-spirit to return, and now walk between the living and the restless dead. Greatly improved in 2nd Edition.
*Themes: death & second chances · debts · unfinished business.*

**Beast: The Primordial (CofD)**
You're a living primal nightmare, feeding on fear and kin to all monsters. The franchise's most controversial line.
*Themes: the monster within · fear & hunger · self-justification.*

**Deviant: The Renegades (CofD)**
The survivor of an experiment that Remade you — now hunting the Conspiracy that did it. Sharp revenge-thriller premise.
*Themes: vengeance · found family · body horror · purpose vs. self-destruction.*

**Mummy: The Resurrection (oWoD)**
An undying Egyptian immortal (Amenti) reincarnated to serve Ma'at against corruption, reborn each time you die. Ambitious but the least-supported of the lines.
*Themes: immortality as duty · cosmic order · the long memory.*
