*A quick-pick guide. Find what you want at the table, then start there.*

---

### Start with the FEEL you want

- **Easiest buy-in / most support & community** → Vampire: The Masquerade
- **Anger, action, and apocalypse** → Werewolf: The Apocalypse
- **Big philosophical ideas** → Mage (Ascension = freedom · Awakening = structure)
- **Grounded, human, relatable** → Hunter: The Reckoning (5e) or The Vigil
- **Raw grief & emotional horror** → Wraith: The Oblivion
- **Spy-thriller & double lives** → Demon: The Descent
- **Bittersweet and beautiful** → Changeling: The Dreaming
- **Pure tragedy** → Promethean: The Created
- **Deep time & ancient duty** → Mummy (Curse / Resurrection)
- **Hidden gems that punch above their popularity** → Promethean, Wraith & Changeling: The Lost

---

### Classic (oWoD) vs. Chronicles (CofD) — pick a universe

**Choose Classic / oWoD if you want:** rich fixed metaplot, ancient history, iconic factions, the biggest fanbase, and current 5th-edition support (Vampire, Werewolf, Hunter).

**Choose Chronicles / CofD if you want:** cleaner unified mechanics, a build-your-own-metaplot sandbox, and access to unique concepts with no Classic equivalent (Promethean, Deviant, Beast).

> **Rule of thumb:** New to the hobby? Start with **Vampire: The Masquerade 5th Edition**. Want to play a regular person? Start with **Hunter: The Reckoning 5th Edition**. Want the most emotionally intense game in the franchise? Pick up **Wraith: The Oblivion** — just bring an experienced GM.
