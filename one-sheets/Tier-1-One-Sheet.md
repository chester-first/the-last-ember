# TIER I ONE-SHEET — THE MARROWMOOR
**Material Plane · Levels 1–4 · ~4–6 sessions** — *the dead won't rest.*
Full detail: [`Tier-1-The-Marrowmoor.md`](../Tier-1-The-Marrowmoor.md) · Boss: [`Boss-One-Sheet.md`](Boss-One-Sheet.md)

## Must happen
1. **Bond the legacy items** at the Barrow-Altar (R3). Items wake — Kindled tier.
2. **Free (or fail) Sera** at R7. Revenant ally; Tether starts 7/10.
3. **Witness Halthurax alive** (R9b) — a scene, NOT a fight.

## Flow
Lantern's Rest (town) leads to 3 Moor leads, into the Marrowmoor Vault (R1–R9), to the Herald boss, then the Goldwyrm sighting, then the molt.

## Boss — the Herald (CR 3)
See the Boss sheet for the Goldwyrm.
- **Touch of the Hoard:** +5, 3d6 necrotic + max-HP reduction until long rest.
- **Gray Sermon (rech 5–6):** 30 ft, DC 13 Wis or *frightened* + 2d8 psychic.
- **Reinforce the Anchor:** +10 HP/round UNLESS the party emptied R8 Hoard-Cells. Reward mercy.

## Clocks this tier
- **Ember 12 → 11** if the party scatters 4+ motes for speed. Show the choice every undead.
- **Court meter:** ±1 with Warden Greel (heed him = +, mock/burn = −).
- **Sera's Tether:** starts 7.

## The mercy choice (run it patiently)
Every undead is a stolen soul. **Return** (action + DC 10) sends it home and slows Ember; **Scatter** (just kill) is fast but lost forever. The doorstep husband Edon and the Mourn-house child are the tutorials — make them sad, not scary.

## Read-aloud cue (R9b, the sighting)
> *"The mirror shows an eye, gold and ancient and big as a cathedral door… 'Go home, little embers. Grow. I can wait.'"*

Aim for awe, not a TPK. If attacked, he restrains and leaves.

## Loot

- Warden's Gray Cloak
- Lanternlight Censer
- Herald's Scale-Shard (plot proof for Tier II)

## End of tier
Level 4; legacy items hit **Burning** entering Tier II. Sera feels a pull "toward the deep woods" — the fey crossing.
