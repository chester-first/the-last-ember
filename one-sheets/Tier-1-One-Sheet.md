## ✦ THE MERCY CHOICE (run it patiently)
Every undead = a stolen soul. **Return** (action + DC 10) sends it home (slows Ember); **Scatter** (just kill) is fast but lost forever. The doorstep husband Edon and the Mourn-house **child** are the tutorials — make them sad, not scary.

## ✦ READ-ALOUD CUE (R9b, the sighting)
> *"The mirror shows an eye, gold and ancient and big as a cathedral door… 'Go home, little embers. Grow. I can wait.'"* — awe, not a TPK. If attacked, he restrains and leaves.
