# Tier I — The Marrowmoor
##### Material Plane · Levels 1–4 · ~4–6 sessions · *"the dead won't rest."*

The party are nobodies in a fog-bound borderland where the dead have begun walking home to their own front doors. They bond their soul-forged items, free the revenant knight Sera, stop the Herald's anchoring rite — and, for one un-fightable moment, meet the grieving god behind it all.

## Must Happen
1. **Bond the legacy items** at the Barrow-Altar (R3). *Items wake — Kindled tier.*
2. **Free (or fail) Sera** at R7. *Revenant ally; Tether starts 7/10.*
3. **Witness Halthurax alive** (R9b) — a scene, **NOT a fight**.

## Clocks This Tier
- **Ember 12 → 11** if the party scatters 4+ motes for speed. Show the choice on every undead.
- **Court meter ±1** with **Warden Greel** (heed him = +, mock/burn = −).
- **Sera's Tether** starts at **7**.

## The Mercy Choice
Every undead is a stolen soul. **Return** (action + DC 10) sends it home and slows the Ember; **Scatter** (just kill) is fast but lost forever. The doorstep husband Edon and the Mourn-house **child** are the tutorials — make them *sad, not scary.*

{{descriptive
The mirror shows an eye, gold and ancient and big as a cathedral door… *"Go home, little embers. Grow. I can wait."*

Run R9b as **awe, not a TPK.** Halthurax (CR 17) will not fight; if attacked he restrains, lectures, and leaves.
