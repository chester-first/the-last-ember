# Tier II — The Verdant Pyre
##### Feywild · Levels 5–10 · ~6–9 sessions · *"the wellspring is blighting."*

The party cross into the Feywild to find the wellspring of light itself going the gray of old ash. They lock the Court between Keepers and Tallymasters, weigh a bargain of real power for real corruption, drop Sera's tether at her patron's grave, and hunt the Dragon-Wraith to its Pyre's Heart — where it molts into a dracolich and tears a soul-fall into the Shadowfell.

## Must Happen
1. **Lock the Court** (Keepers ↔ Tallymasters). *Its allegiance pays off in the Tier IV finale.*
2. **Offer the Tallymasters' Bargain** — real power for real corruption. **No gotcha.**
3. **Drop Sera's Tether** (auto −1 from the Pull); let her confront her patron's grave.

Required social offer, lvl 7–8. Vol offers each PC **ONE**: **+1 ability (max 20)** / an early Burning power / **40 temp HP per long rest** this tier.
