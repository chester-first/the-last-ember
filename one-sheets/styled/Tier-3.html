Tier III — The Hoard-Kingdom

Shadowfell · Levels 11–16 · ~7–10 sessions Player death is expected
“he has built a kingdom.”

The dead don't want saving. Ossuara is a city at dusk, pleasant and full, and its souls refuse freedom. The party recruits at the Death-Court, watches Sera's fate-vector lock when she meets her old liege, breaks the Siege of the Pale Hoard, and ends a dracolich in his Throne-Vault — then molts toward the Source. Full detail: Tier-3-The-Hoard-Kingdom.md.

Must Happen

  1. The dead don't want saving. Ossuara is pleasant; its souls refuse freedom. Sit in it.
  2. Sera's fate-vector LOCKS when she meets her old liege, Chancellor Lord Vant.
  3. The dracolich fight — run it lethal; the Ember Clock crisis (4–2) turns on here.
The Flow

Ossuara opens onto the Grateful Dead, the Death-Court (where you can recruit Kalaurith), Sera's reckoning, the Siege of the Pale Hoard, and the Throne-Vault boss, then the molt to the Source.

The Grateful Dead

The moral gut-check — no combat. Souls the party "returned" earlier are HERE and angry — including the Mourn-house child. How full Ossuara is depends on the Ember Clock: healthy Clock = most rekindled and absent; drained Clock = full of the party's good intentions. Make the number mean it.

Sera's Reckoning

Locks her ending:

  • Tether 7+ & backed: reclaims her name → toward FREED.
  • Tether 3–6: spends self to win → toward MARTYR.
  • Tether 0–2: Vant reclaims her → DEFECTS to Halthurax.

Clocks This Tier

  • Ember → crisis (4–2): death gets full teeth — resurrection costs another's mote or returns the PC Touched.
  • Sera Tether: drops at tier break; her reckoning sets the vector.

You brace for a charnel pit. You get a city at dusk… “No one dies here. No one leaves here. He keeps us all.” She says it like a blessing. She believes it is one.

Loot

Ossix's Pale Blade ◈ · Kalaurith's Scale (if allied) · 3× Soul-Pyre Cores ◈ · Chancellor's Seal.

End of Tier

Level 16; items hit Radiant entering Tier IV. The soul-stream now runs upward to the Source. A PC lost here may return whole at the Source.
