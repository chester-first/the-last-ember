# Tier III — The Hoard-Kingdom
##### Shadowfell · Levels 11–16 · ~7–10 sessions · *"he has built a kingdom."* · **Player death is expected.**

The dead don't want saving. Ossuara is a city at dusk, pleasant and full, and its souls refuse freedom. The party recruits at the Death-Court, watches Sera's fate-vector lock when she meets her old liege, breaks the Siege of the Pale Hoard, and ends a dracolich in his Throne-Vault — then molts toward the Source.

## Must Happen
1. **The dead don't want saving.** Ossuara is *pleasant*; its souls refuse freedom. Sit in it.
2. **Sera's fate-vector LOCKS** when she meets her old liege, **Chancellor Lord Vant.**
3. **The dracolich fight** — run it lethal; the Ember Clock crisis (4–2) turns on here.

## The Grateful Dead
The moral gut-check — **no combat.** Souls the party "returned" earlier are HERE and **angry** — including the Mourn-house child. *How full Ossuara is depends on the Ember Clock:* healthy Clock = most rekindled and absent; drained Clock = full of the party's good intentions. **Make the number mean it.**

## Sera's Reckoning
Locks her ending:
- **Tether 7+ & backed:** reclaims her name → toward **FREED.**
- **Tether 3–6:** spends self to win → toward **MARTYR.**
- **Tether 0–2:** Vant reclaims her → **DEFECTS** to Halthurax.

## Clocks This Tier
- **Ember → crisis (4–2):** death gets full teeth — resurrection costs another's mote or returns the PC **Touched.**
- **Sera Tether:** drops at tier break; her reckoning sets the vector.
