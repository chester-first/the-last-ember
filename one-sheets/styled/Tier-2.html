Tier II — The Verdant Pyre

Feywild · Levels 5–10 · ~6–9 sessions
“the wellspring is blighting.”

The party cross into the Feywild to find the wellspring of light itself going the gray of old ash. They lock the Court between Keepers and Tallymasters, weigh a bargain of real power for real corruption, drop Sera's tether at her patron's grave, and hunt the Dragon-Wraith to its Pyre's Heart — where it molts into a dracolich and tears a soul-fall into the Shadowfell. Full detail: Tier-2-The-Verdant-Pyre.md.

Must Happen

  1. Lock the Court (Keepers ↔ Tallymasters). Its allegiance pays off in the Tier IV finale.
  2. Offer the Tallymasters' Bargain — real power for real corruption. No gotcha.
  3. Drop Sera's Tether (auto −1 from the Pull); let her confront her patron's grave.
The Flow

Glimmermarch leads through the Graying Glades to the Court Vote and the Bargain, then the Dragon-Wraith hunt and the Pyre's Heart boss, then the molt to a dracolich.

Dragon-Wraith (Form II)

Huge undead (was metallic dragon), CR 13 · full block on Boss sheet
Armor Class 18   Hit Points 212   Speed 40 ft., fly 80 ft.
Graying Breath (Recharge 5–6). 60-ft cone, DC 17 Con, 10d8 necrotic + max-HP reduction. Relit founts grant advantage vs. it.
Soul Siphon. Heals when it drops a creature → protect the fallen.
Court Payoff. Redeemed → Aurene + 4 fey knights aid at initiative 10. Corrupted → the party fights alone.

The Bargain

Required social offer, lvl 7–8. Vol offers each PC ONE: +1 ability (max 20) / an early Burning power / 40 temp HP per long rest this tier.

Vol's offer is real. Court −3, Ember −1, and the party has now personally hoarded souls. Let it be real power that costs the world.

Clocks This Tier

  • Ember 11 → 10; the "Dimming" threshold (8) turns on → Raise/Revivify costs +1 Ember segment.
  • Court meter LOCKS here (glades + vote + Bargain).
  • Sera Tether: patron's grave +2 (done well); the Pull auto −1.

Color floods in — grass that glows, rivers of light running uphill… and the far ridge gone the gray of old ash, where nothing drifts at all.

Cost beat (lvl 9): Pip Emberkin (pixie guide) dies buying the party time, or a PC eats a lasting max-HP cut. Make it land.
