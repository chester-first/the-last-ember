## Tone dial — set the table mood

- **Gothic politics & seduction** → Vampire (Masquerade / Requiem)
- **Rage, action, eco-horror** → Werewolf (Apocalypse / Forsaken)
- **Cerebral, philosophical, weird** → Mage (Ascension / Awakening)
- **Grounded paranoia & desperation** → Hunter (Reckoning / Vigil)
- **Raw grief & inner conflict** → Wraith: The Oblivion
- **Spy-thriller & hidden identity** → Demon: The Descent
- **Faith, damnation & fallen grace** → Demon: The Fallen
- **Bittersweet wonder** → Changeling: The Dreaming
- **Captivity & trauma horror** → Changeling: The Lost
- **Quiet tragedy & longing** → Promethean
- **Death, debts & the underworld** → Geist
- **Ancient duty & deep time** → Mummy (Curse / Resurrection)
- **Self-justifying predation** → Beast
- **Vengeance thriller / body horror** → Deviant

> **Mixing lines?** Classic and Chronicles cosmologies don't share rules or metaphysics — pick one universe per chronicle. Within a universe, cross-line play (e.g., Vampire + Werewolf + Mage) is supported but raises power-balance and tone-clash issues. Decide the dominant mood first, then invite the others into it.
