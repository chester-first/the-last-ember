## ✦ BOSS — DRACOLICH (Form III, CR 21) → full block on Boss sheet
- **Distributed Phylactery:** +30 HP/turn **UNLESS the 3 Soul-Pyres are down.** *The Siege pre-kills some — reward it.*
- **Hoardfire (rech 5–6):** 90-ft cone, DC 22 Dex, 16d6 necrotic+fire.
- **Call the Grateful Dead:** a hoarded citizen bodyblocks an attack → his "innocents" as shields.

## ✦ THE GRATEFUL DEAD (the moral gut-check, no combat)
Souls the party "returned" earlier are HERE and **angry** — including the Mourn-house child. *How full Ossuara is depends on the Ember Clock:* healthy Clock = most rekindled and absent; drained Clock = full of the party's good intentions. **Make the number mean it.**

## ✦ SERA'S RECKONING (locks her ending)
- **Tether 7+ & backed:** reclaims her name → toward **FREED.**
- **Tether 3–6:** spends self to win → toward **MARTYR.**
- **Tether 0–2:** Vant reclaims her → **DEFECTS** to Halthurax.

## ✦ CLOCKS THIS TIER
- **Ember → crisis (4–2):** death gets full teeth — resurrection costs another's mote or returns the PC **Touched.**
- **Sera Tether:** drops at tier break; her reckoning sets the vector.
