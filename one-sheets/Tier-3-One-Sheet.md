# TIER III ONE-SHEET — THE HOARD-KINGDOM
**Shadowfell · Levels 11–16 · ~7–10 sessions** — *he has built a kingdom.* **Player death is expected.**
Full detail: [`Tier-3-The-Hoard-Kingdom.md`](../Tier-3-The-Hoard-Kingdom.md) · Boss: [`Boss-One-Sheet.md`](Boss-One-Sheet.md)

## Must happen
1. **The dead don't want saving.** Ossuara is *pleasant*; its souls refuse freedom. Sit in it.
2. **Sera's fate-vector LOCKS** when she meets her old liege, Chancellor Lord Vant.
3. **The dracolich fight** — run it lethal; the Ember Clock crisis (4–2) turns on here.

## Flow
Ossuara (city) into the Grateful Dead, the Death-Court (recruit Kalaurith), Sera's reckoning, the Siege of the Pale Hoard, the Throne-Vault boss, then the molt to the Source.

## Boss — Dracolich (Form III, CR 21)
Full block on the Boss sheet.
- **Distributed Phylactery:** +30 HP/turn UNLESS the 3 Soul-Pyres are down. The Siege pre-kills some — reward it.
- **Hoardfire (rech 5–6):** 90-ft cone, DC 22 Dex, 16d6 necrotic + fire.
- **Call the Grateful Dead:** a hoarded citizen bodyblocks an attack — his "innocents" used as shields.

## The Grateful Dead (the moral gut-check, no combat)
Souls the party "returned" earlier are HERE and **angry** — including the Mourn-house child. How full Ossuara is depends on the Ember Clock: a healthy Clock means most are rekindled and absent; a drained Clock means it's full of the party's good intentions. Make the number mean it.

## Sera's reckoning (locks her ending)
- **Tether 7+ & backed:** reclaims her name, toward FREED.
- **Tether 3–6:** spends self to win, toward MARTYR.
- **Tether 0–2:** Vant reclaims her, DEFECTS to Halthurax.

## Clocks this tier
- **Ember → crisis (4–2):** death gets full teeth — resurrection costs another's mote or returns the PC Touched.
- **Sera Tether:** drops at tier break; her reckoning sets the vector.

## Read-aloud cue (Ossuara)
> *"You brace for a charnel pit. You get a city at dusk… 'No one dies here. No one leaves here. He keeps us all.' She says it like a blessing. She believes it is one."*

## Loot
- Ossix's Pale Blade
- Kalaurith's Scale (if allied)
- 3× Soul-Pyre Cores
- Chancellor's Seal

## End of tier
Level 16; items hit **Radiant** entering Tier IV. The soul-stream now runs upward to the Source. A PC lost here may return whole at the Source.
