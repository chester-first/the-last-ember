# TIER II ONE-SHEET — THE VERDANT PYRE
**Feywild · Levels 5–10 · ~6–9 sessions** — *the wellspring is blighting.*
Full detail: [`Tier-2-The-Verdant-Pyre.md`](../Tier-2-The-Verdant-Pyre.md) · Boss: [`Boss-One-Sheet.md`](Boss-One-Sheet.md)

## Must happen
1. **Lock the Court** (Keepers vs. Tallymasters). Its allegiance pays off in the Tier IV finale.
2. **Offer the Tallymasters' Bargain** — real power for real corruption. No gotcha.
3. **Drop Sera's Tether** (auto −1 from the Pull); let her confront her patron's grave.

## Flow
Glimmermarch (hub) into the Graying Glades, to the Court Vote and the Bargain, then the Dragon-Wraith hunt, the Pyre's Heart boss, then the molt to dracolich.

## Boss — Dragon-Wraith (Form II, CR 13)
Full block on the Boss sheet.
- **Graying Breath (rech 5–6):** 60-ft cone, DC 17 Con, 10d8 necrotic + max-HP reduction. Relit founts grant advantage vs. it.
- **Soul Siphon:** heals when it drops a creature — protect the fallen.
- **Court payoff:** redeemed gives Aurene + 4 fey knights at init 10; corrupted means the party fights alone.

## The Bargain (required social offer, lvl 7–8)
Vol offers each PC ONE: **+1 ability (max 20)**, an early Burning power, or **40 temp HP per long rest** this tier.
**Cost:** Court −3, Ember −1, and the party has now personally hoarded souls. Let it be real power that costs the world.

## Clocks this tier
- **Ember 11 → 10**; the **Dimming threshold (8)** turns on, so Raise/Revivify costs +1 Ember segment.
- **Court meter LOCKS** here (glades + vote + Bargain).
- **Sera Tether:** patron's grave +2 (done well); the Pull auto −1.

## Read-aloud cue (arrival)
> *"Color floods in — grass that glows, rivers of light running uphill… and the far ridge gone the gray of old ash, where nothing drifts at all."*

## Cost beat (lvl 9)
**Pip Emberkin** (pixie guide) dies buying the party time, or a PC eats a lasting max-HP cut. Make it land.

- 2nd Scale-Shard

## End of tier
Level 10; items hit **Blazing** entering Tier III. The molt tears a soul-fall into the Shadowfell.
